About Me

I am a computer science student with an interest in philosophy and effective altruism. My academic interests revolve around formal systems and theoretical computer science, I particularly enjoy Theory of Computation. My current goal is eventual employment in AI alignment.

In the programming realm, I favor functional languages like Haskell and OCaml for a variety of reasons. So much so that one of the major reasons I use NixOS as my daily computer is that I can build my entire OS using a purely functional build language.

Beyond academics I enjoy reading, writing essays on a variety of topics that interest me, browsing the web generally, and watching UFC.
